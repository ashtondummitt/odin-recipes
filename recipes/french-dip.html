French Dip Sand-O

Succulent braised beef isn't as difficult as it sounds. Stick it between some store bought bread, dip it it the jus, take a bite. Do it.

Ingredients

For the BEEF

  • 5 Yellow Onions - Sliced Thin
  • 2 TBSP Butter
  • Shit ton of Minced Garlic
  • 2-10lb Chuck Roast
  • Enough Beef/Veal Stock

For the Rest

  • Store Bought Hoagie Rolls
  • Havarti Cheese

Technique

  1. Season Chuck Roast with a generous amount of salt (secret ingredient, unlisted).
  2. Heat nuetral oil in dutch oven large enough to fit roast. Medium high heat. Sear roast on all sides. Set aside.
  3. Add butter to dutch oven over medium heat. Add onions. Caramelize those jons. Add garlic. Do not burn. Burnt garlic sucks.
  4. Scrape and fond from dutch oven and return the roast. Add strock.
  5. Secure lid and place in a 350 degree farenheit oven for four hours or more. Beef should be fork tender.
  6. Strain and reserve jus and onions seperatly.
  7. Assemble sandwich with cheese on top. Toast, dip, and enjoy.
